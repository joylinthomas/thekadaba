The Kadaba Tax Payers & Tax Savers Credit Co-operative Society

The Tax Payers and Tax Savers Credit Co-operative Society Limited (Kadaba) is a localized financial institution that provides specialized banking and credit services specifically for its members in the Kadaba region. Unlike typical mutual benefit societies that focus on general community welfare, this society is designed as a strategic financial hub aimed at helping individuals manage their personal finances, optimize their tax liabilities, and grow their wealth.

The society offers a range of secured investment and credit products including Fixed Deposits (FDs), Recurring Deposits (RDs), and Monthly Income Schemes (MIS), alongside quick-access credit facilities like Gold Loans and Property Loans. By operating as a registered credit cooperative under the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, it bridges the gap between traditional banking and tax-saving consultancy, ensuring that member funds are utilized for secured internal lending while providing higher interest returns on savings compared to standard commercial banks.
